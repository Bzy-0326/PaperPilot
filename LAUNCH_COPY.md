## Short Launch Post

I built **Paper Reader**, a paper recommendation product that tries to solve a practical research problem:

- what should I read first
- why is this paper worth attention
- which paper is more realistic to reproduce

Instead of showing a raw paper feed, it turns a topic into ranked cards, one-sentence takeaways, detail pages, comparison views, and follow-up lists.

It supports:

- Docker deployment
- bring-your-own API key onboarding
- optional local Ollama inference
